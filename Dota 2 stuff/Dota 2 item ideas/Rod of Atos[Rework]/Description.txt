Old Mechanic:
	Launches a projectile towards a target which if connects, the target is
	rooted for 2 seconds.
	
New Mechanic:
	Launches a slightly faster but much more visible & audible projectile 
	towards a target. Upon hit, the target is stunned for 0.6 seconds and 
	then rooted for 1.5 seconds. Enemies surrounding the target are 
	knocked away 350 units from the target.
