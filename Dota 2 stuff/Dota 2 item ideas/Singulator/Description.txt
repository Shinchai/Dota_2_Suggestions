Name: Singulator

Requirements:

	Magic Staff(1000)
	Shadow Amulet(1300)
	Perserverance(1700)
	
Total cost: 4000 gold
	
Abilities:

	Active: Seclusion
	Turns an enemy completely invisible to his allies for 4 seconds.
	If the enemy dies while under this effect, its killer will become invisible
	for 6 seconds with 15% bonus movespeed.
	Allies lose complete track of the affected unit(no vision, map marker, etc)
	
	Cooldown: 28 seconds
	Manacost: 140
	

	+10 Intelligence.
	+20 Attack Speed.
	+6 HP Regen.
	+1.25 Mana Regen.
