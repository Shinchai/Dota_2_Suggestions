Name: Twin Swords

Requirements:
	Broadsword(1200) x2
	Recipe(350)

Total cost: 2750
	
Abilities:

	Passive: Flurry
		Gain attack speed buff with 5 stacks. While stacks are available,
		gain +50 attack speed. 1 stack is lost per hit attack, all stacks are
		lost upon a missed attack. Gain 1 stack every 2.5 seconds.
		
		Multiple instance of this passive do not stack.
	
	
	+36 Damage.
